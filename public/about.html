About Rasikh Academy

Our Mission

At Rasikh Academy, our mission is to empower individuals and organizations with the technological expertise required for excellence in the digital age. We achieve this through specialized, high-quality training programs tailored to meet diverse needs across various sectors.

Our Vision

To be a leading technology training partner, enabling digital transformation and fostering technological self-sufficiency across diverse sectors and communities.

Core Values

  • Excellence: We are committed to delivering the highest standard of education and training, ensuring our programs meet the diverse needs of all learners.
  • Relevance: Our focus is on imparting skills and knowledge that are directly applicable to the evolving demands of various sectors, ensuring our learners are well-prepared for real-world challenges.
  • Security: We uphold the highest standards of data protection and confidentiality, safeguarding the privacy and trust of all individuals and organizations we engage with.
  • Innovation:We continuously evolve our curriculum to address emerging technologies and methodologies, fostering a culture of creativity and forward-thinking.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency and ethical standards in all partnerships, building trust and accountability in every interaction.

Our Expertise

Rasikh Academy brings together leading experts in technology education, with extensive experience across various sectors. Our instructors include:

  • Former technology advisors with a wealth of experience in strategic planning and digital transformation.
  • Industry-certified security professionals committed to upholding the highest standards of data protection and confidentiality.
  • Experienced software engineers with a proven track record in delivering complex projects across diverse industries.
  • Educators specialized in computer science and professional development, dedicated to fostering a culture of continuous learning.
  • Technology training specialists with expertise in adult learning methodologies, ensuring effective and engaging learning experiences for all participants.

Our Approach

At Rasikh Academy, we employ a learner-centered training methodology designed to equip professionals across various sectors with the skills necessary for success in the digital age. Our approach includes:

  • Needs assessment to identify organizational skill gaps
  • Customized Curriculum Development
  • Multi-modal delivery options including on-site, virtual, and hybrid formats
  • Practical, Project-Based Learning
  • Continuous Evaluation and Improvement
